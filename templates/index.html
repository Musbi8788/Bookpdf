Title: Never Split The Difference

Author: Chris Voss

Never Split the Difference by Chris Voss teaches negotiation techniques
based on his experience as an FBI hostage negotiator.
The book emphasizes that traditional methods, like compromise,
often lead to poor results. Instead, Voss introduces strategies like mirroring,
tactical empathy, and asking calibrated questions to build trust,
understand others, and achieve better outcomes in both professional and personal negotiations.
The focus is on getting what you want without compromising your goals.
